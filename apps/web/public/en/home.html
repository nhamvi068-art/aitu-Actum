Frequently asked questions

If you are looking for an AI image generation workspace or an open-source AI whiteboard, these answers cover the basics.

  • How is Actum different from a regular AI chat tool?

    AI chat tools are great for conversations. Actum is built for execution: generated images, prompts, notes, diagrams, and assets stay together on a reusable canvas.

  • What AI image tasks can Actum support?

    Actum is useful for covers, posters, character concepts, product visuals, social media images, inspiration boards, and multi-version visual exploration.

  • How does DeepSeek-v4 fit into Actum?

    DeepSeek-v4 can help expand prompts, draft copy, break down requirements, plan storyboards, and structure ideas before the results are organized on the canvas.

  • Is Actum open source?

    Yes. Actum is open source and can be self-hosted. You can also use the hosted workspace online and review the source code on GitHub.
